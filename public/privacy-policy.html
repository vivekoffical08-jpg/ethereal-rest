Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Ethereal Rest ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our Ethereal Rest application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Sleep Data: Duration, quality, and patterns of your sleep sessions recorded through the app.
  • Device Data: Accelerometer and microphone sensor data used only during active sleep sessions for tracking purposes.
  • Phone Number: If you enable WhatsApp notifications, we collect your phone number to send sleep reminders and reports.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including screens visited and features used.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Track and analyze your sleep patterns to provide insights
  • Send WhatsApp notifications and sleep reminders (only if you enable this)
  • Improve the app experience and fix bugs
  • Generate sleep trend reports visible only to you

4. WhatsApp Integration

If you choose to enable WhatsApp notifications, your phone number is shared with Meta Platforms (WhatsApp) solely for the purpose of delivering sleep-related notifications. We do not sell or share your phone number with any third parties for marketing purposes.

You can disable WhatsApp notifications at any time from the Profile settings screen.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your sleep data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion.

We retain your data only as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with:

  • Google Firebase — for secure data storage
  • Meta (WhatsApp) — only if you enable WhatsApp notifications
  • Google Gemini AI — for generating sleep insights (data is anonymized)

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for WhatsApp notifications at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Ethereal Rest is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
